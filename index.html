Safety4Kids

Safety4kids is a new community interest company set up to help young people moving schools or location to avoid trouble and stay safe on the streets. We have all read the shocking stories of young people being targeted by gangs and bullies and this is confirmed by the recent Youth Endowment fund report-Children, Violence and Vulnerability.

Our aim is to give young people the self-confidence and knowledge to be aware of issues and to avoid trouble. We work in schools and community centres in south London providing free courses to vulnerable and disadvantaged children.
