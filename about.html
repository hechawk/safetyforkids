Safety4Kids


In 2022 we ran a programme in 3 schools called 'Kids at Risk' with Young Mums Support Network, but have now decided to set up a new venture to deal solely with the issue of staying safe on the streets:

Safety4kids CIC

Jason is the operations director, responsible for delivering the courses; he has taught in over 90 schools in the UK, providing young people with the ability to be aware of trouble and to avoid it. Jason is a mixed martial arts instructor, but our focus is avoiding trouble not engaging with it! The courses run for 5/6 weeks during school term time in school hours and consist of a weekly one hour session, which is designed to discuss issues that are of most concern to the participants within a structured format that covers a range of topics. The discussion is supported, where appropriate with written material from Jason's book and access to a video. This is followed by 30 minutes of physical activity, where participants learn agility and how to avoid trouble. We are happy to provide separate sessions for parents to participate and share their concerns, as well as provide feedback on their children's reaction to the learning experience.
